Description

Oh no! The burglar is at it again dropping bombs on an empty field! It is up to you, three disconnected buckets, to catch all of the bombs before they blow up! Be careful though, each round the speed and number of bombs increases! If you miss a bomb one of your buckets will disappear. The only way to get it back is to score 1000 points.

Controls

  • Left click: start the round
  • Right click: open the menu
